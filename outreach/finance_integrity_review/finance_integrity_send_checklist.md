# Finance Integrity Review Send Checklist

Date created: 2026-06-27
Status: Draft v0.1
Use: Complete before any first-send outreach.

## Before Sending

- [ ] Confirm company is a plausible finance workflow target.
- [ ] Confirm contact role is relevant.
- [ ] Confirm no duplicate outreach already exists.
- [ ] Confirm first-send copy uses approved language.
- [ ] Confirm no ZIP is attached.
- [ ] Confirm no source code is attached.
- [ ] Confirm no protected method details are included.
- [ ] Confirm no free pilot is offered.
- [ ] Confirm no training rights are granted.
- [ ] Confirm no code transfer is implied.
- [ ] Confirm no broad exclusivity is implied.
- [ ] Confirm production validation is not claimed.
- [ ] Confirm the email says paid, bounded pilot if pilot is mentioned.
- [ ] Confirm Proton/manual-send workflow is used.

## Approved First-Send Material

Use:

`outreach/finance_integrity_review/finance_integrity_first_send_email.md`

Optional source packet for reference only:

`docs/specshift_core/finance_integrity_review_pilot_packet.md`

Do not attach the internal packet unless deliberately approved.

## Send Record

After sending, update:

`outreach/finance_integrity_review/finance_integrity_outreach_tracker.csv`

Minimum fields to update:

- Company
- Contact name
- Contact role
- Contact email or channel
- Source
- Date prepared
- Date sent
- Material sent
- Attachment sent
- Status
- Follow-up due
- Next action

## Follow-Up Rule

First follow-up:

3-5 business days after first send.

Second follow-up:

7-10 business days after first follow-up.

Park after second follow-up unless there is a clear buyer signal.

## Stop Conditions

Stop or pause if the buyer asks for:

- Protected method.
- Code transfer.
- Training rights.
- Broad exclusivity.
- Free pilot.
- Legal terms.
- Procurement terms.
- Security review.
- Data rights.
- Money movement.

Those trigger careful review and likely CPA/legal support before commitments.
