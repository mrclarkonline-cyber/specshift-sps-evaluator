# Client Phase 1 Scope and Boundary Registration

Status: complete  
Date: 2026-06-28

## Purpose

Client Phase 1 defines the safety boundaries for the SpecShift Client Intake + Delivery System.

## Allowed workflow

- initial scoping conversation
- client workflow identification
- artifact intake checklist
- public/private/protected classification
- bounded review packet creation
- candidate-discrepancy memo drafting
- human-review controlled delivery
- closeout note with limitations and next-step options

## Prohibited uses

- weapons, targeting, lethal autonomy, kinetic operations, battlefield command, or offensive cyber
- rights-violating surveillance or identity targeting
- autonomous production monitoring
- unreviewed compliance certification
- truth validation claims
- legal advice
- financial advice
- medical advice
- binding contract terms
- client action instructions without human review

## No-free-pilot guardrails

No free pilots by default.

A pilot needs written scope, boundaries, acceptance criteria, and payment terms.

## Professional-support threshold

If procurement, paid pilot, contract terms, budget, or money movement becomes real, engage CPA/legal support before proceeding alone.

## Boundary

This is workflow scope only.

It does not create legal advice, financial advice, binding contract terms, compliance certification, production monitoring, truth validation, or autonomous client action.
