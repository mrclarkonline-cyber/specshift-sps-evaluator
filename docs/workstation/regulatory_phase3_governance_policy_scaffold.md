# Regulatory Phase 3 Governance and Policy Scaffold

Status: complete  
Date: 2026-06-28

## Purpose

Create governance-policy scaffolds for future regulatory, certification, insurer, and procurement diligence.

## Policy scaffolds created

- prohibited use
- human review
- data handling and retention
- protected methods
- claim safety
- professional review triggers

## Core governance position

SpecShift outputs are:

- candidates
- scaffolds
- review prompts
- indexes
- audit records
- delivery structures

SpecShift outputs are not:

- findings
- truth determinations
- certifications
- legal conclusions
- financial conclusions
- autonomous instructions

## Boundary

These are governance-readiness scaffolds only.

They are not legally binding policies, legal advice, financial advice, tax advice, certification, compliance approval, procurement approval, production monitoring, or truth validation.
