# Professional Phase 3 Financial and Contract Thresholds

Status: complete  
Date: 2026-06-28

## Core rule

If procurement, paid pilot, contract terms, budget, or money movement becomes real:

**Engage CPA/legal support before proceeding alone.**

## No-free-pilot posture

No free pilots by default.

Safe posture:

“I can discuss a bounded paid pilot with written scope, success criteria, data boundaries, and human-review controls.”

## Paid pilot must have

- written scope
- systems in scope and out of scope
- artifact/data boundaries
- human-review responsibility
- buyer-retained labels and final adjudication
- acceptance criteria
- timeline
- fee/payment terms
- confidentiality and non-use terms
- IP/protected-method boundary
- limitation of liability
- no production monitoring claim
- no truth-validation claim

## Lawyer review questions

- public/private claim safety
- NDA/MSA/SOW/pilot terms
- liability, warranties, indemnity, limitation of liability
- prohibited-use language
- protected methods and trade secrets
- client data retention and confidentiality
- IP ownership
- first-send versus post-NDA materials

## CPA review questions

- revenue recognition
- bookkeeping system and chart of accounts
- owner contributions and reimbursements
- expense tracking
- tax reserves
- out-of-state or international client issues
- pilot/license/consulting revenue classification

## Boundary

This is an internal professional-readiness scaffold only.

It is not legal advice, financial advice, tax advice, valuation advice, binding contract language, or an offer.
