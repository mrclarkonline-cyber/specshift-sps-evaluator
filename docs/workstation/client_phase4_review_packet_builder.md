## Review packet boundary

A review packet is not a finding.

It is a structured human-review scaffold.

## Candidate-discrepancy memo boundary

A candidate-discrepancy memo scaffold does not assert that a discrepancy exists.

It preserves review questions, limitations, and human adjudication.

## Boundary

This phase does not create truth validation, compliance certification, production monitoring, legal advice, financial advice, or autonomous client action.
