# Client Intake Questionnaire

Status: scaffold  
Date: 2026-06-28

## Purpose

This questionnaire identifies whether a buyer-safe observable-only pilot is in scope.

It is designed to confirm:

- what workflow the buyer wants reviewed
- what observable traces the buyer can export
- who controls labels and adjudication
- what must not be sent
- whether procurement, contract, payment, licensing, exclusivity, IP, or data-transfer thresholds have been triggered

## 1. Buyer contact and authority

- Who is the pilot sponsor?
- Who is authorized to approve scope?
- Who owns the workflow under review?
- Who will provide human adjudication?
- Who can approve data transfer and retention terms?

## 2. Workflow scope

- What workflow should be reviewed?
- What business or operational risk makes this workflow worth reviewing?
- What visible final state or claimed completion matters?
- What systems are in scope?
- What systems are explicitly out of scope?
- What timeframe or sample window should be used?

## 3. Observable trace exports

- What observable workflow traces can the buyer export?
- What fields are present in the trace export?
- Are event timestamps included?
- Are actor/system identifiers pseudonymized?
- Are claimed final states included?
- Are intermediate visible states included?
- Are failed, abandoned, or ambiguous cases available?

## 4. Labels and ground truth

- Does the buyer have labels or ground truth?
- Who owns the labels?
- How were labels created?
- Will labels remain buyer-controlled?
- Is an ambiguous or insufficient-information category allowed?
- Who performs final adjudication?

## 5. Pilot boundaries

Confirm:

- No model weights will be shared.
- No source code will be shared.
- No private prompts will be shared.
- No hidden activations will be shared.
- No private chain-of-thought will be shared.
- This is not a production-monitoring request.
- Final decisions remain human-controlled.

## 6. Delivery and review

- Who receives candidate-discrepancy memos?
- Who reviews limitations?
- What delivery format is preferred?
- What timeline is desired?
- What would count as useful evidence for deciding whether to continue?
- What would count as a no-go or stop condition?

## 7. Professional threshold

Answer yes/no:

- Is procurement involved?
- Are contract terms being discussed?
- Is this a paid pilot?
- Is licensing or exclusivity being discussed?
- Will any data transfer agreement, NDA, MSA, SOW, DPA, or security review be required?
- Will money movement occur?

If yes to any of these, engage qualified legal and CPA support before proceeding alone.

## Boundary

This is a buyer intake scaffold only.

It does not request protected internals and does not create legal advice, financial advice, binding terms, compliance certification, production validation, automated verdicts, free-pilot commitments, or autonomous client action.
