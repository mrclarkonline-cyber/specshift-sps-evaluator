# Non-Retention and Boundary Statement

Status: scaffold  
Date: 2026-06-28

## Default posture

Do not retain client artifacts beyond what is necessary for the scoped review unless written terms say otherwise.

## Non-retention rules

- Do not retain raw client traces by default after the scoped review period unless written terms authorize retention.
- Do not use client artifacts for model training.
- Do not use client artifacts to improve unrelated systems without written authorization.
- Do not transfer client artifacts to third parties without written authorization.
- Do not include client artifacts in public examples, outreach, demos, or marketing materials without explicit written authorization.
- Do not treat buyer labels or ground truth as SpecShift-controlled assets.
- Preserve only minimal provenance records required for auditability, unless written terms require deletion or different retention.

## Minimal records that may be appropriate

- artifact name or project identifier
- classification
- received date
- hash where appropriate
- review status
- delivery status
- deletion/return status
- human-review notes approved for retention

## Requires written terms

- retaining client artifacts
- using client artifacts in future demonstrations
- sharing artifacts externally
- using artifacts for training or product improvement
- publishing case studies
- granting exclusivity
- assigning IP
- accepting payment or paid pilot terms

## Boundary

This is an internal scaffold for professional review.

It is not legal advice, a binding data processing agreement, a privacy policy, a security certification, or a contract term.
