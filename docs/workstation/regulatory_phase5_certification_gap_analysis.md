# Regulatory Phase 5 Certification Gap Analysis

Status: complete  
Date: 2026-06-28

## Purpose

Create a gap analysis against likely certification, regulator, insurer, procurement, and standards-based diligence expectations.

## What exists

SpecShift has meaningful internal readiness scaffolding:

- governance-policy scaffolds
- control evidence inventory
- procurement due-diligence packet index
- professional readiness package
- client intake/delivery scaffold
- provenance and claim-safety gates
- audit records

## Largest current gaps

- No formal certification or conformity assessment exists.
- No ISO/IEC 42001 or equivalent management-system audit exists.
- No formal security questionnaire/access-control packet exists.
- No adopted lawyer-reviewed policies or customer contract terms exist.
- No professional compliance, tax, accounting, valuation, or legal opinion exists.

## Safe overall claim

SpecShift has meaningful internal readiness scaffolding and evidence inventory.

SpecShift is not certified, compliant, government-approved, procurement-approved, or professionally audited.

## Boundary

This is a gap analysis only.

It does not claim certification, compliance, government approval, procurement approval, legal sufficiency, financial/tax approval, production monitoring, professional audit, or truth validation.
