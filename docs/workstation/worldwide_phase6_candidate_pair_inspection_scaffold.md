## Phase 6 goal

Phase 6 inspects candidate pairs at the metadata level only.

It checks:

- both sources have provenance
- both sources are in the same category
- source-type diversity is labeled
- uncertainty labels are preserved
- claim-safety labels are preserved
- claim boundaries are preserved
- whether later human review would be meaningful

## Boundary

Phase 6 does not claim:

- production monitoring
- content validation
- contradiction detection
- agreement detection
- corroboration
- alert readiness
- autonomous action
- truth resolution
