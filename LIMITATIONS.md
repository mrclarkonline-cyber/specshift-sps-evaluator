# Limitations

SpecShift SPS Evaluator is a public synthetic scaffold intended for demonstration and review purposes.

Current limitations include:

- The scaffold does not execute generated code.
- Runtime-only failures are outside scope.
- The dataset is synthetic and small-scale.
- The task distribution is not validated against production generated-code systems.
- Adversarial cases are synthetic constructions for demonstration purposes.
- No production-scale benchmarking has been performed.
- No false-positive rate has been formally characterized on real-world datasets.
- Reviewer-lift observations discussed publicly are AI-mediated synthetic demonstrations only.
- Candidate discrepancies are reviewer-facing observations for human adjudication and are not automated verdicts or correctness proofs.
