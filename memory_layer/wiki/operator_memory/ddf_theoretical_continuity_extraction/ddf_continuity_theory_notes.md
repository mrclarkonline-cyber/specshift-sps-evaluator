## Operating Boundary

This page preserves the theoretical continuity layer without converting it into metaphysical, legal, moral, or commercial proof.

The continuity papers are useful for explaining why first-order success is not enough, but they should not be used to claim consciousness, selfhood, personhood, or live production validation.

## Core Theoretical Claim

Performance, stability, and continuity are separable.

A system may preserve apparent output success while losing internally organized correction structure.

## Continuity Frame

Continuity is not merely persistence of signal.

Continuity means the system preserves structured correction across perturbation, rather than merely replaying, forcing, or passively maintaining the same visible output.

## Why ∆∆F Matters Here

First-order measures ask:

> Did the system succeed?

Second-order trajectory measures ask:

> How did the system’s process of adjustment change while succeeding?

The continuity layer uses ∆∆F / κ to examine whether correction structure remains organized over time and under perturbation.

## Safe Interpretation

Use:

> Owned continuity is a diagnostic construct for persistent, structured, state-contingent correction under perturbation.

Avoid:

> Owned continuity proves consciousness or selfhood.

## Key Distinctions

### Performance

What the system achieves.

Failure mode:

- replay
- environmental simplicity
- memorized output
- external forcing
- frozen policy that still performs under easy conditions

### Stability

Whether the observable trajectory remains bounded, smooth, or low-error.

Failure mode:

- externally forced smoothness
- passive equilibrium
- stable output without active organization

### Continuity

Whether correction structure remains organized, endogenous, recurrent, and perturbation-sensitive.

Failure mode:

- scrambled correction
- replayed control
- imposed action sequence
- state-independent forcing
- apparent success without owned correction

## Safe Use Cases

This theoretical layer can support:

- AI evaluation language
- adaptive-system review

- SpecShift’s design instinct that observable process matters

## Unsafe Use Cases

Do not use this layer to claim:

- consciousness detection
- legal personhood
- moral status
- subjective experience
- autonomous intent
- production reliability
- physics validation
- clinical or governance authority
