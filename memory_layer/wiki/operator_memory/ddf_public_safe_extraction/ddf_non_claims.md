## Non-Claims

### 1. Not consciousness detection

Do not claim:

> ∆∆F detects consciousness.

Safe replacement:

> ∆∆F tests observable second-order trajectory structure under perturbation.

### 2. Not intent detection

Do not claim:

> ∆∆F infers intent.

Safe replacement:

> ∆∆F may flag candidate structure in observed correction dynamics for reviewer interpretation.

### 3. Not moral or legal status

Do not claim:

> ∆∆F determines moral status, legal status, personhood, sentience, or rights.

Safe replacement:

> ∆∆F is a bounded diagnostic signal, not a normative or legal verdict.

### 4. Not hidden-mechanism reconstruction

Do not claim:

> ∆∆F reveals the internal mechanism.

Safe replacement:

> ∆∆F can be applied without mechanism access, but that also limits causal interpretation.

### 5. Not universal agency proof

Do not claim:

> ∆∆F proves agency absolutely.

Safe replacement:

> ∆∆F may distinguish tested forms of state-conditional correction from specified nulls under bounded conditions.

### 6. Not production validation

Do not claim:

> ∆∆F is validated for live production failure prediction.

Safe replacement:

> ∆∆F has diagnostic and design-layer value; live operational value requires external validation under realistic workloads.

### 7. Not SpecShift commercial proof

Do not claim:

> SpecShift is commercially validated because of ∆∆F.

Safe replacement:

> ∆∆F is intellectual provenance. SpecShift’s commercial value remains contingent on buyer-controlled blind validation.

### 8. Not physics proof

Do not claim:

> ∆∆F proves new physics, validates a unified field theory, enables vacuum control, or establishes warp feasibility.

Safe replacement:

> Some documents explore interpretive or phenomenological extensions, but these are not established physics or commercial validation.

### 9. Not an automated verdict

Do not claim:

> The diagnostic decides whether a system is safe, reliable, authentic, or adaptive.

Safe replacement:

> The diagnostic returns candidate structure for human review under a specified protocol.

### 10. Not a replacement for governance or science

Do not claim:

> ∆∆F replaces audit, science, clinical judgment, safety engineering, or governance.

Safe replacement:

> ∆∆F can support structured review when used with controls, falsification criteria, and human adjudication.

## Master Boundary Sentence

∆∆F / κ is an observable second-order trajectory diagnostic, not a consciousness detector, intent detector, legal-status test, hidden-mechanism extractor, physics proof, or production-readiness guarantee.
