## Highest-Value Canon Candidates

### 1. Observable-only diagnostic framing

Promote as core:

- ∆∆F / κ is an observable second-order trajectory diagnostic.
- It should be tested under intervention, perturbation, and negative controls.
- It does not require access to hidden internals.
- It does not infer consciousness, moral status, legal status, subjective experience, or intent.
- It is falsifiable if the null/scrambled-control surrogate matches the observed curvature distribution and temporal structure within tolerance.

### 2. Separation claim

Promote carefully:

- Performance, stability, and continuity are distinct.
- A system can preserve first-order success while losing correction structure.
- First-order performance alone is not sufficient to identify adaptive authority or owned continuity under perturbation.

### 3. Scramble-control null

Promote as a central test pattern:

- Compare observed trajectory curvature against a scrambled-control or randomized directional null.
- Preserve marginal statistics where possible.
- Destroy temporal, causal, or state-conditional structure.
- Treat failure against the null as meaningful and diagnostic-limiting.

### 4. Public replication posture

Promote:

- Public-facing documents can include empirical diagnostics, controls, reproducible summaries, figures, and falsification criteria.
- Proprietary or mechanism-level details should remain undisclosed unless deliberately reviewed.

### 5. Risk and containment posture

Promote internally:

- Some documents are boundary-drawing and non-operational.
- Risk materials should not be converted into deployment pathways.
- High-risk physics or governance material should remain archive-caution unless reframed conservatively.
