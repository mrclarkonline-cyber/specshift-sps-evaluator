Candidate Skin Registry v0.1
Internal candidate-skin registry. Not buyer-facing copy.

Core product: SpecShift Architectural Logic Diagnostics
First offer: Focused Diagnostic Pilot

Purpose:
Register additional candidate vertical skins without turning them into standalone products or built packs.

Standing boundary:
These are candidate skins for a pre-deployment diagnostic and architecture-stage review process. They are not benchmarks, certifications, compliance audits, runtime monitors, security tools, deployment approvals, professional advice, or guarantees of AI safety.

Build rule:
Do not build these into full v0.1 packs unless buyer workflow evidence points to one of them.

Candidate skins:

1. Legal Operations Workflow Diagnostic Pack
Status: candidate skin only, not built
Buyer problem: Legal-ops agents may mishandle intake, privilege-sensitive context, contract routing, matter ownership, or draft-final boundaries.
Safe use case: Pre-deployment review of legal-operations workflow support, contract intake routing, or matter triage.
Likely failure families: provenance confusion, professional boundary overreach, handoff ownership gap, false final-state claim, scope overextension
Excluded uses: legal advice, contract approval, privilege determination, compliance certification, deployment approval
