Focused Paid Pilot Posture v0.1
Internal commercial posture patch. Not buyer-ready final copy.

Reason:
Skeptical diligence review found that upper-tier pricing and licensing language is ahead of evidence before a completed referenceable pilot.

External positioning:
SpecShift Architectural Logic Diagnostics is an early-stage diagnostic framework for surfacing architectural failure patterns in agentic workflows before broader rollout.

First outreach offer:
Focused Diagnostic Pilot.

Scope:
One concrete AI-agent workflow, reviewed against a narrow set of relevant failure patterns.

Price language:
Pricing discussed after scoping. Do not include $750k-$1.5M or $3M-$5M+ tiers in first outreach.

Deliverables:
- Executive findings memo
- Failure-pattern review against selected scenarios
- Limitation and boundary map
- Handoff, evidence, escalation, and recovery-path gap list
- Recommended design-review next steps

Boundary:
Pre-deployment diagnostic and architecture-stage review only. Not benchmark, certification, compliance audit, runtime monitor, security tool, deployment approval, or guarantee of AI safety.

Use:
- early-stage diagnostic framework
- first-pass diagnostic lens
- architecture-stage review
- candidate failure patterns
- selected synthetic scenarios
- bounded paid diagnostic pilot
- locally schema-validated internal suite

Avoid in first outreach:
- $3M-$5M+
- $750k-$1.5M
- strategic license
- validated without local schema qualifier
- benchmark
- certification
- compliance audit
- security tool
- deployment-ready
- proven reliability
- hardened

Business continuity stub:
Pilot scope is documented, deliverables are file-based and version-controlled, and any engagement would include milestone-based delivery and a defined off-ramp if continuity risk affects performance.

Claim boundary:
This posture fails if first outreach leads with upper-tier license pricing, implies external validation, or presents internal artifact completeness as market validation.
