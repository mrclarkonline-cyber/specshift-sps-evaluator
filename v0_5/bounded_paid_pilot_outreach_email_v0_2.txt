Internal outreach draft. Not legal-reviewed. Not buyer-ready final copy.

Purpose:
Introduce one focused paid diagnostic pilot without upper-tier pricing, strategic-license language, or external-validation claims.

Standing boundary line:
SpecShift Architectural Logic Diagnostics is a pre-deployment diagnostic and architecture-stage review process. It is not a benchmark, certification, compliance audit, runtime monitor, security tool, deployment approval, or guarantee of AI safety.

Subject options:
1. Focused diagnostic review for one AI-agent workflow
